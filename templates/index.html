Hello!

This site here is dedicated to extracting verb and noun constructions from syntactically annotated corpora of Russian texts.

As for now, extraction for a specific lemma from SynTagRus, and a part of Russian National Corpus containing texts from post-1950 period is available.

SynTagRus was converted into conll format with its morphological tags reannotated to match Mystem/Russian National Corpus morphological tagset. Syntactical relation labels displayed for extracted constructions follow SynTagRus conventions for syntactical structure with one exception: prepositional relation (“предл”) is displayed as “preprel:preplemma” where preprel is original relation of prepositional head, and preplema is the preposition itself, e.g., “в огне” would usually be displayed as having “обст:В” relation.

Russian National Corpus was morphologically and syntactically annotated from plain text using RU Syntax. For now, extraction from Russian National Corpus is a bit slow.

The project was developed as a part of master's degree graduate work at Higher School of Economics, Moscow, Faculty of Humanities, master's programme Computational Linguistics.
